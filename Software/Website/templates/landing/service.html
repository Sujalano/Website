School Management Features

Student Information System (SIS): Centralized database for enrollment, digital profiles, and historical academic records.
Automated Attendance Tracking: Real-time logging via mobile app or biometric integration with instant SMS alerts to parents.
Smart Gradebook & Examination: Automated result calculation, customizable report card generation, and performance analytics.
Fee & Finance Management: Secure online payment gateways, automated invoice generation, and pending-dues reminders.
